Server Guidelines

Our Community Expectations

Be Respectful

  • Treat every member with the kindness and respect you expect in return.
  • Consider the feelings and personal boundaries of others, refraining from offensive or harmful behavior.
  • Maintain a respectful tone and communication style to foster a positive environment.

Be Smart

  • Avoid any form of spamming; keep your communication meaningful and relevant.
  • Prior to posting or speaking, take a moment to think, read, and listen attentively.
  • Contribute constructively to discussions using common sense and thoughtful insights.
  • Help keep the server organized and clean for the benefit of all users.

Act in Good Faith

  • Refrain from bringing external drama into the server; address internal matters respectfully and privately.
  • Advertising within the server or via direct messages (DMs) is prohibited unless you have obtained prior permission from Staff.

Guiding Principles

Create a Welcoming Space: The goal is to foster a friendly and respectful environment where members can engage in meaningful conversations and establish genuine connections.

Contact Staff for Assistance: If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our approachable staff members.

Protection of Personal Information

  • No Sharing of Personal Details: Under no circumstances should you share your personal information within the server. This includes any identifiable details about yourself.
  • Limited Liability for Information Sharing: Neither the server nor its administrators hold any liability for consequences arising from personal information sharing.
  • No DOXing: The act of disclosing personal information about individuals (referred to as DOXing) will result in an indefinite ban from the server and report to Discord.

This list does not constitute the full set of rules, and our Moderators may take action on misbehavior and violations that are not explicitly listed above. At all times, use common sense and good judgment for anything you do on the server.
